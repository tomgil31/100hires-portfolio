# AI-Powered SEO Content Production Playbook

## Objective

Create a repeatable workflow for researching SEO experts and extracting actionable insights using AI.

---

## Step 1 — Select an Expert

Choose an SEO professional with proven experience in AI search, content strategy, or enterprise SEO.

Examples:

- Kevin Indig
- Lily Ray
- Rand Fishkin

---

## Step 2 — Collect Content

Gather information from:

- YouTube
- LinkedIn
- Official Blog
- Podcast Interviews

---

## Step 3 — Download Transcripts

Use the YouTube Transcript API to collect video transcripts for analysis.

---

## Step 4 — Analyze Content

Perform keyword frequency analysis to identify recurring topics such as:

- AI
- Google
- Search
- SEO
- Content
- Brand
- Traffic

---

## Step 5 — Generate AI Summary

Use Google Gemini to summarize:

- Main ideas
- Strategies
- Best practices
- Emerging trends

---

## Step 6 — Organize Findings

Store research inside:

- experts/
- linkedin-posts/
- youtube-transcripts/
- analysis/
- reports/

---

## Step 7 — Compare Experts

Compare:

- Common themes
- Unique strategies
- AI adoption
- Content recommendations

---

## Outcome

The final output is a structured knowledge base that can be reused to support AI-powered SEO content production, research, and strategic planning.
