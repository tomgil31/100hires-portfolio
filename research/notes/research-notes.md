# Research Notes

## Project Topic

AI-Powered SEO Content Production

## Key Patterns Observed

- AI search is changing how users discover content.
- SEO strategy is shifting from only ranking-focused work to broader visibility and authority.
- Brand credibility is becoming more important in AI-powered search results.
- High-quality expert content is still valuable for building trust.
- Transcript analysis helps identify repeated themes across expert discussions.

## Common Themes

- AI search
- Google updates
- Content strategy
- Brand authority
- Organic visibility
- SEO measurement

## Research Method

The project used a combination of:

- YouTube Data API
- YouTube transcript extraction
- LinkedIn manual review
- Gemini AI report generation
- Markdown-based documentation

## Takeaway

The research supports the idea that AI-powered SEO content production should combine automation, expert review, and structured analysis rather than relying only on AI-generated content.
