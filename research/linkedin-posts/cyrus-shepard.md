## Notes

Make sure the bots can actually get in: The absolute number one factor is "URL Accessibility." If your page is hiding behind a paywall, blocking AI crawlers in the backend, or throwing errors, you're invisible. The AI literally cannot cite what it can't reach. 

 Old-school SEO is still the backbone: Winning traditional Google search is still a massive deal. If you are already ranking high for a main topic—and especially if you rank for all the related side-questions (what they call "fan-out" queries)—the AI is heavily biased to pull your content for its answer. 

 Spoon-feed the AI: AI models don't want to dig through a wall of text. They want direct, factual answers right near the top of the page. You need to structure your content with clear headings and match the format to the question (like using a bulleted list if the user asks for the "best options") so the AI can extract the info without having to think too hard.
