## Notes

It’s officially live: Barry Schwartz caught that Google is finally rolling out the "Further Exploration" section at the bottom of their AI Overviews. They teased this update a while back, and now it's actually showing up in everyday searches.

The "Recommended Reading" list: Think of it as the syllabus or "where to look next" section after a quick AI summary. Once the AI gives you the basic answer, this feature drops links to deeper articles so you can explore the topic from different angles.

Pushing past the TL;DR: Google says the main goal is to keep us curious. Instead of just grabbing the quick AI summary and bouncing, they want to push us to click through to real websites to get actual human perspectives and deeper research.
