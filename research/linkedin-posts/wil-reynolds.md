## Notes
ChatGPT is actively searching for specific brands: Wil pointed out that the new ChatGPT 5.5 isn't just scraping generic web pages. When a user asks a broad question, the AI is doing "query fan outs"—meaning it runs secondary searches behind the scenes specifically looking for trusted brand names to help build its answer.

Brand reputation is the new SEO: This is exactly why the industry is suddenly obsessed with "building a brand" for AI. If the language model already associates your company with a specific topic, it will intentionally seek you out rather than just grabbing whatever generic site ranks at the top of traditional Google search.

The proof is in the data (and you need to check yours): He backed this up by showing how ChatGPT is doing this with his own agency's website. His main takeaway is a warning: you need to look at your own industry right now to see if ChatGPT is actively searching for your brand, or if it is searching for your competitors instead
