## Notes 
Real Experience Beats AI: Don't just hire a random freelance writer or use ChatGPT to pump out generic content. If you need an article on unclogging a toilet, you need an actual plumber to write it. One incredible, expert-backed article is worth more than a hundred average AI posts.

Stop Overthinking Keywords: Traditional keyword research is getting too competitive. Instead, go straight for "Bottom of the Funnel" (BOFU) keywords—the things people search right before they make a decision, like "Tools for X," "Alternatives to X," and "X Pricing." 

Create, Don't Just Regurgitate: The future of search belongs to websites that actually generate new information and insights. You can't win by simply reorganizing what is already on the internet; you have to build something people genuinely want to talk about, and you have to invest in good design so it looks as good as it reads.
