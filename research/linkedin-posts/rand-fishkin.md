## Notes
The Massive Traffic Wipeout: Stanica tracked 100 blogs that were crushing it back in 2022 (each making over $100k/year). Four years later, the results are brutal: the median blog lost 85% of its organic search traffic. That means half of these highly successful sites did even worse, with many losing over 99% of their traffic or dropping to absolute zero. Only 21 of the 100 managed to actually grow.

The Cause of the Crash: The blogs that got destroyed were mostly relying on basic "what is" or "how-to" articles. With the rise of Google's AI Overviews and Helpful Content Updates, search engines stopped sending traffic to sites that just provided generic information, because the AI can easily summarize those answers right at the top of the search page. 

who Survived (and Why): The sites that actually maintained or grew their traffic had one thing in common: direct, real-world experience. The survivors published content that an AI literally cannot fake or summarize from somewhere else—like firsthand product testing, unique brand perspectives, and content built for a dedicated, loyal audience rather than just chasing random search clicks.
